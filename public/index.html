TAMIL TAMLISH ENGLISH
Learning Tamil using English

The purpose of this website is to teach Colloquial (Spoken) Tamil to tourists, using the teaching method known as transliteration (teaching foreign language using another language's writing system/script - in this case English)

Tamil is a language that dates back over 2 millenia and has 2 distinct dialects - Colloquial and Literary Tamil. As tourists wish for the most part to be able to communicate with and understand the locals, only Colloquial Tamil needs to be taught, to a basic level.

Example conversation between a tourist and a local
Lessons

Covering a variety of suitable topics relevant for tourists, and utiliises teaching methods backed both by research and proven in the field in existing applications
Interactive activities

Including listening, reading and writing exercises, as well as many other acitvties such as mutliple choice and pictoral based questions
Accessibility

Contrasting colours, as well as easy navigation and simple understandable features
Exposure

All Colloquial Tamil spoken by a local, and content is based off knowledge surrounding what tourists would experience in a foreign country.

This project is the artefact for my EPQ (Extended Project Qualification), that I am undertaking as part of my A-Levels

Some of the main motivations for this project for me is the fact that I have famiy in India, who converse in Tamil and also how I myself have experienced the problem of being in a foreign country and not being able to understand the language.
